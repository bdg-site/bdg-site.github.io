Letter of Reference & Recommendation

To Whom it May Concern:

I’m writing to unconditionally endorse and recommend Steve Brown as a phenomenal custom home builder. Steve recently worked with my husband and I to build our first custom home; We moved in August, 2013. The longer we live in the house, the more we’ve come to appreciate both the quality of the home he created for us and our family, as well as the exceptional value that we got.

Steve was easy to work with throughout the process. He’s accessible, and always puts in long hours to make sure deadlines get met. Like a taskmaster, he hounds his subcontractors to put quality workmanship into every corner of the home, and to get it done on time.

The City of Boulder tried to insert new hurdles and requirements into our project at every turn, and Steve expertly helped us navigate the red tape to keep our project on time and within budget. By the final inspection, the building inspector had been won over too—we were glad to hear from his own mouth that it was the “best built house [he’d] seen in over a decade as an inspector.”

Steve’s attention to detail and creativity are inspiring, and are the qualities that elevate him from not just a “good” builder, but to a “great” builder in my opinion. Steve met and exceeded every expectation of ours: In everything from expertly finding and combining the best materials, to listening to our wants and needs and bringing them to life, to adding surprise bells-and-whistles to the house that we never could have imagined.

Without hesitation, I highly recommend Steve Brown to you as a custom home builder. A Brown-built house is not just one that you will enjoy for years, but is also one that will make you the envy of your neighbors.

Please feel free to call or email any time if you have any other questions about Steve or his work for us.

Sincerely,
